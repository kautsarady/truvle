Truvle Quiz is a LINE BOT Games that simple, fun, and can be played in group, multiple chat, or 1 on 1 chat. Quiz consist of a clue and the puzzle (shuffled word) that must be solved in 15 seconds. Score is given for those who can solve the puzzle, 10 point for the first person who guessed right, 5 point for the second person, 4 point for the third person, and so and so. The scores will be accumulated at the end of the quiz, all points gain by each player will be shown.
